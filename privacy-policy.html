Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how ToolsPack Online handles information when you use our website and free online utilities.

1. Local Data Processing

Our tools (text converters, JSON formatters, password generators, encoders, hash utilities, and similar tools) process all data you enter entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. No text, code, passwords, or JSON you type is sent to, stored on, or processed by our servers.

2. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (measurement ID: G-Y4ZTSC0V0R) to understand how visitors use our website. This service collects anonymized data such as pages visited, time spent on site, browser type, device type, and general geographic location (country/city level).

Google Analytics does not collect the content you enter in our tools. It tracks website usage patterns only. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Cookies

Google Analytics may use cookies to distinguish unique visitors and track session data. These cookies do not contain personal information or the content you enter in our tools. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

4. Server Logs

Our hosting provider may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages requested, timestamps) for security and performance monitoring. We do not link this data to the content you enter in our tools.

5. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party sites.

6. Children's Privacy

ToolsPack Online is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

This policy is effective as of June 2026. For more about our project, visit the About page.
