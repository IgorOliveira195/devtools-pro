About ToolsPack Online

Our Mission

ToolsPack Online was created to provide practical, everyday web utilities that anyone can use without compromising their privacy. We believe that simple tools — converting text case, formatting JSON, generating passwords — should not require uploading your data to a remote server or creating an account.

Every tool on this site is designed to run entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. What you type, paste, or generate stays on your device.

What We Offer

ToolsPack Online currently provides seven free utilities:

  • Text Case Converter — Transform text to uppercase, lowercase, title case, or inverse case
  • JSON Formatter — Prettify, minify, and validate JSON strings
  • Password Generator — Create strong random passwords with cryptographically secure randomness
  • Base64 Encoder & Decoder — Encode and decode Base64 strings with UTF-8 support
  • URL Encoder & Decoder — Percent-encode and decode URL strings and query parameters
  • Hash Generator — Compute SHA-256, SHA-384, and SHA-512 hashes
  • Word Counter — Count words, characters, sentences, and lines in real time

Each tool includes detailed guides, practical examples, and frequently asked questions to help you use it effectively.

Privacy by Design

Privacy is not an afterthought — it is the foundation of how we build tools. Here is what that means in practice:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No server-side processing of your input data
  • No storage or logging of text, JSON, or passwords you work with
  • No selling or sharing of user data with third parties

We use Google Analytics to understand general traffic patterns (pages visited, browser type, country). This data is anonymized and does not include the content you enter in our tools. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

Who We Are

ToolsPack Online is an independent web project focused on building useful, privacy-respecting online tools. We are a small team of developers who use these tools ourselves in daily work — debugging APIs, formatting data, and managing credentials securely.

We prioritize quality over quantity. Rather than offering hundreds of shallow utilities, we focus on a curated set of tools with thorough documentation and reliable functionality.

Our Values

  • Privacy first — Your data belongs to you, not to us
  • Simplicity — Tools should be intuitive and work instantly
  • Transparency — Open about how our tools work and what data we collect
  • Quality — Reliable tools with helpful documentation, not bare-minimum widgets

Looking Ahead

We plan to expand ToolsPack Online with additional utilities based on user needs — always maintaining our commitment to client-side processing and privacy. New tools will follow the same standard: functional, well-documented, and respectful of your data.
